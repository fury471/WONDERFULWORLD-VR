# Sandbox

Each teammate should use their own folder and personal scene for experiments.

Current demonstration direction:

- use sandbox scenes to prove attraction logic, scenic connectors, and soft-boundary tricks before they enter the shared park scene

## Naming suggestion

- `Sandbox_Xuanyuan.unity`
- `Sandbox_Tongyan.unity`
- `Sandbox_Haobo.unity`
- `Sandbox_Wenao.unity`
- `Sandbox_YuFu.unity`

## Rules

- Prototype freely here.
- Do not treat sandbox scenes as production scenes.
- Once a feature works, convert it into prefabs and move reusable content into `Features` or `World`.
- Sandbox scenes are also the right place to fake open-world feel through landmarks, atmosphere, and boundary illusions before shared integration.
